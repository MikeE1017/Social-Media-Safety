How to Be Safe on Social Media

Why Social Media Safety Matters

Social media is an incredible way to connect with friends, family, and even strangers across the globe. However, it can also put you at risk if you are not careful.

Hackers, scammers, and people with bad intentions can use your posts and personal information against you. Learning how to protect yourself is the first step toward a safer online experience.

Tips for Staying Safe

  • Use strong and unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
  • Think twice before sharing personal information.
  • Review and update privacy settings regularly.

Steps to Protect Your Account

  1. Update your passwords every few months.
  2. Turn on login alerts to be notified of suspicious logins.
  3. Always log out when using a shared computer or device.
  4. Report fake accounts or suspicious messages immediately.
